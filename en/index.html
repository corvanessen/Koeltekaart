About this map

Koeltekaart Leiden is an interactive map of cool spots in Leiden during hot weather. You'll find drinking water points, shaded parks, free outdoor swimming water, paid swimming pools and cool indoor locations such as libraries and community centres. The map also shows live temperature readings via sensorleiden.nl and the current weather forecast for Leiden. Filter by category in the sidebar to quickly find a cool spot near you.

Categories

Data collected from municipal sources, drinkwaterkaart.nl, OpenBomenKaart, OpenStreetMap and sensorleiden.nl (live temperature sensors). Locations, opening hours and readings may change.
